intended to help startup players with a quick overview of maybe relevant cards

methodology

based on 2306 public decklists published on NRDB between Jan 13, 2022 and Jan 13, 2023

faction lists are based on in-faction cards appearing in that faction's decks. only the top 50% shown

limitations:

  • does not take ban lists into account, so some banned cards appear in the lists
  • pure popularity contest, no fancy analysis or expert meta knowledge applied

hopefully useful as a starting point
